About FreeT

FreeT helps you stay on top of all your digital subscriptions and free trials in one place. Easily detect what you're subscribed to, receive alerts before you're charged, and never forget to cancel an unused service again.

Why We Ask for Permissions

To help you track your subscriptions automatically, FreeT can (with your permission) access your iOS subscriptions and scan your email for relevant transaction receipts. Your privacy is our top priority — no data is sold, shared, or stored remotely.
