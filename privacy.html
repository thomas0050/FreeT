Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 13, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Subscription Tracker (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (“App”).

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information, only with your explicit consent:

  • OAuth Access: Access tokens for Gmail, Outlook, or other email providers, to retrieve relevant transaction and subscription emails. We do not access your full inbox or store emails permanently.
  • Apple Subscription Data: Information from your iOS device about App Store subscriptions using StoreKit. This is local to your device.
  • Manually Entered Data: Subscriptions you add manually (service name, cost, renewal date, etc.) are stored locally.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous statistics such as screen views and crash logs (optional).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To detect, categorize, and manage your subscriptions and free trials
  • To remind you of upcoming renewals or trial expirations
  • To improve app functionality and user experience

3. Data Storage and Retention

We do not store your email content, access tokens, or subscription data on our servers. All data is stored securely on your device. If cloud backup is enabled on your device (e.g. iCloud), your data may be backed up per your device’s settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Data accessed via Gmail or Microsoft APIs is used solely for the purpose of identifying subscriptions and is handled in compliance with Google’s API Services User Data Policy.

5. Your Rights

  • You may revoke OAuth access at any time via your Google or Microsoft account settings.
  • You may delete all app data via the in-app settings screen.
  • You may request full deletion of all usage data (if collected) by contacting us.

6. Compliance

We comply with applicable data privacy regulations including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and Google’s user data policies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, the updated date will be reflected at the top of this page.

8. Contact

For questions about this policy, contact us at: support@yourappdomain.com
