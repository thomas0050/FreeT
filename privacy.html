Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 13, 2025

Subscription Tracker takes your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • OAuth credentials to access your email (Gmail, Outlook, etc.), only with your explicit consent
  • Subscription data found in emails or via iOS system APIs
  • Anonymous app usage statistics (optional, no personal info)

How We Use Your Information

Your subscription and email data is used only to help you track and manage your subscriptions. We do not share, sell, or store this data on our servers. All data is processed locally or with trusted APIs (Google, Microsoft).

Your Rights

You can revoke access at any time via your Google or Microsoft account settings. You can also delete your local data from the app settings.
